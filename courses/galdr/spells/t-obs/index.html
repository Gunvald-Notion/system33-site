Galdr Spell · Kap 9 + 10

Tobs — Distance in Wobbles

The gap between a claim and a handful, measured in the bell's own ruler. One number tells you: forkast or behold.

Distance only means something when you state the unit. 12 000 kroner is meaningless. It could be enormous or trivial. 12 000 kroner in a field where the typical wobble is 6 000 is two wobbles. Two wobbles is a coordinate you can read on the bell.
Which test are you running?
σ kjent — z-test. Wobble: σ/√n. Critical value from z-table.
σ ukjent — t-test. Wobble: s/√n. Critical value from t-table, df = n−1.
μ ≠ μ&sub0; — tosidig (two-tailed). Rejection in both tails, α/2 each.
μ > μ&sub0; or μ < μ&sub0; — ensidig (one-tailed). All α in one tail.

Decision RuleKap 9–10
|Tobs| > tcrit → Forkast H0   |   |Tobs| ≤ tcrit → Behold H0
Forkast = reject the claim. Behold = keep believing until stronger evidence arrives. Behold ≠ accept.
The Three RunesKeep separate
σ (truth)  ≠  s (estimate)  ≠  s/√n (wobble of x̄)
σ is the real spread. s estimates it. s/√n is how much x̄ wobbles. Tobs counts wobbles, not sigmas.
