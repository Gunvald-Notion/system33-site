Formula Reference — Kap 5 + 6
E[X] — Expected ValueKap 5
Σ xi · P(xi) = μ
The center. Weighted average of all values. Every other formula measures from here.
Var[X] — VarianceKap 5
Σ (xi − μ)2 · P(xi)
Average squared distance from center, weighted by probability. How spread the values are.
σ — Standard DeviationKap 5
σ = √Var[X]
Square root of variance. Back to original units.
Cov(X,E) — CovarianceKap 6
ΣΣ (xi − μX)(ej − μE) · P(xi, ej)
Do X and E move together? Walk every cell, check direction, weight, pile up.
ρ — CorrelationKap 6
ρ = Cov(X,E) / (σX · σE)
Covariance scaled to [−1, 1]. Removes unit dependency. Pure direction signal.
