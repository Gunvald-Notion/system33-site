Formula Reference — Kap 2 + Kap 4

The five named formulas from the book. Each one is a way of reading P-values from the filled house.

Komplement Kap 2
P(A′) = 1 − P(A)
Read from the Σ rad margin. The simplest conservation law readout — what's left when you subtract P(A) from 1.
Addisjon Kap 2
P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A∩B)
Three rooms gathered. P(A) from the row margin, P(B) from the column margin, P(A∩B) subtracted because it was counted in both.
Multiplikasjon Kap 4
P(A∩B) = P(A|B) · P(B)
The barrier. P(A|B) = P(A∩B) / P(B) — shrink the dome to the B-column, measure A's density inside. Multiply back by P(B) to get the intersection.
Oppsplitting Kap 4
P(B) = P(B|A)·P(A) + P(B|A′)·P(A′)
Total probability. P(B|A) = P(A∩B)/P(A) from the A-lane. P(B|A′) = P(A′∩B)/P(A′) from the A′-lane. Each conditional times its row margin, summed.
Bayes Kap 4
P(A|B) = P(B|A)·P(A) / P(B)
The reversal. P(B|A) = P(A∩B)/P(A). Flip: P(A|B) = P(A∩B)/P(B). Same intersection cell, different denominator from the margins.
