Formula Reference — Kap 2 + Kap 4

The five named formulas from the book. Each one is a readout of the filled house.

Komplement Kap 2
P(A′) = 1 − P(A)
The complement. Read from the Σ rad margin. Already known — the simplest conservation law readout.
Addisjon Kap 2
P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B)
The union. Three rooms of the house gathered together. The overlap subtracted because it was counted twice.
Multiplikasjon Kap 4
P(A ∩ B) = P(A | B) · P(B)
The barrier formula. Shrink the dome to B, measure A inside, scale back. The intersection rewritten as conditional times margin.
Oppsplitting Kap 4
P(B) = P(B|A) · P(A) + P(B|A′) · P(A′)
Total probability. Split the B-column into two lanes weighted by row margins. Used to build P(B) for a Bayes denominator.
Bayes Kap 4
P(A|B) = P(B|A) · P(A) / P(B)
The reversal. You know the forward conditional. Bayes flips the anchor — same intersection, different denominator.
