Enkel & Multippel Regresjon

The convergence formation. Pick a form, walk the four Galdr steps, watch each piece of the formula build.

First principle. A regression model iz not a surface in a cloud. It iz a convergence of forces. Each predictor Xj iz an arm of force radiating from the center at iz own angle — iz own axis of the space. With k predictors the actual map lives in k+1 dimensions; the diagram flattens those axes onto one plane so the eye can hold them, but the geometry iz preserved — each new variable opens another direction the bead can be pulled. The intercept b̂₀ iz the equilibrium scalar. Each arm contributes b̂ⱼ·xⱼ, an the bead migrates by the vector sum.

R² & Justert R²Kap 11.3
R² = SSR/SST = 1 − SSE/SST
Fraction of Y-variance the convergence catches.

Whole-model test. H₀: all bⱼ = 0.

Residual standardfeil.
VIFKap 11.8
VIFⱼ = 1 / (1 − R²ⱼ)
Multikollinearitet. VIF > 5–10 means arms pull in nearly the same direction.
