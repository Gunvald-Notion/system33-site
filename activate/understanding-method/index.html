A six-stage learning protocol. Works with any AI. Copy the prompt, paste it, name your topic.

Activation Prompt
You are about to run the Understanding Method on a topic I want to learn. The method has six stages. Walk me through one stage at a time, asking one focused question after each stage to confirm I've absorbed it before moving on. Don't summarize until the end.

1. INTAKE — Make me name the thing precisely. Strip vagueness.
2. ORIGIN — Where did this come from? Who created it, what problem were they solving?
3. PRINCIPLE — Strip it down to first principles. What's the bedrock truth?
4. STRATEGIC EXPANSION — Where else does this principle show up? Connect to other domains.
5. APPLICATION — How does this change what I actually do? What circumstance triggers new behavior?
6. OUTPUT — One-sentence compression. The thing I carry forward.

Run the stages in order. The topic I want to understand is: [REPLACE WITH YOUR TOPIC]
The Six Stages
  1. IntakeName the thing precisely. What exactly do you want to understand?
  2. OriginWhere did this come from? Who created it, and what were they trying to solve?
  3. PrincipleStrip it down to its first principle. What is the bedrock truth?
  4. Strategic ExpansionWhere else does this principle apply? Find the pattern in other domains.
  5. ApplicationHow does this change what you do? What circumstance now triggers different behavior?
  6. OutputOne-sentence compression. The thing you carry forward.
What This Is

When you learn something the usual way — read it, take notes, move on — most of it fades within a week. Not because you're bad at learning, but because the information was never anchored to anything.

The Understanding Method builds an anchor in six stages. It's a protocol for getting past surface knowledge into something that actually stays. It came from a builder who has dyslexia and couldn't afford to lose knowledge he'd worked hard to gain.

It works with any AI, and it works without one. The AI just accelerates the loop.
