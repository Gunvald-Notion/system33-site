What Activation Means

An activatable method is a protocol that runs. You give it to an AI as a prompt, name your input, and the method walks you through the work. Each method has stages — clear steps with a defined order and a defined output.

Methods aren't theory. They're tools you fire when you need them — like operating system features that activate on demand.
